If you find that it is not working,

please press Ctrl + F5 on that page.


This extension is working well on Windows.

If your OS is any others, and find some functions are not working quiet well,

you can choose which to disable or enable by the ckeckbox below.

Function 1, scoll tab

Hold right click and scroll mousewheel to switch Chrome tab. Scroll up to previous tab, down to next tab.

Function 2, page up/down

Pressing Ctrl and Shift, and then key down PageUp or PageDown, move tab position with its direction. PageUp to previous tab, PageDown to next tab.

Function 3

Pressing Ctrl and Shift, and then key down "{" or "}", popup tab to temp by key down "{". Just popup tab by key down "}".
